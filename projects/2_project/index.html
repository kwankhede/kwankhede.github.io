  • TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS

    We offer strategy consulting and product development services in learning platforms, data science, and machine learning. We partner with organizations in addition to having our own in-house development.

    • Sanghamitra’s foray into engineering products, with our first product: air coolers.

      The introductory range is available in two variants, Uno (duct) and Brio (desert), and five different sizes (2.5ft to 5ft). They are available to be purchased from our brand new store in Wardha, Maharashtra.

      Proven to be superior in quality and durability compared to similar products in the market, our air cooler product was developed from concept to market by our young team (ages 20 to 25) of local talents from Wardha. Each of them engaged in multiple roles as researchers, engineers, and technology/business managers to gain a holistic perspective and skill development.

    • Sanghamitra’s launched its latest product – water filters.

      Built with premium quality materials and a powerful water filtration process backed by data, the water filter range is available in 5 variants with 3 different capacities (10 liters, 13 liters, and 16 liters). They can be purchased from our store in Wardha, Maharashtra.
